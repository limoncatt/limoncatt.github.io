Welcome! I'm a Front-End developer with a growing skill set.

I also have a strong interest in design and am improving my skills to complement my technical abilities. I started programming with Python, drawn by its simplicity and versatility. My first project was a simple Tetris-inspired game that taught me the basics of game logic and design. This success led me to take on bigger projects, including developing a game engine from scratch. Although the engine was lost, the experience was invaluable.

As I grow in my career, I'm excited to see where this journey takes me. Whether creating stunning front-end interfaces, developing complex game mechanics, or designing intuitive user experiences, I'm committed to pushing my limits. Thank you for joining me on this adventure, and I look forward to sharing my work and progress with you.
